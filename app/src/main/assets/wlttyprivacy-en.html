GraspWLT Privacy Protection Agreement

Update Date: 2024-05-29

Effective Date: 2023-10-25

To protect your rights, the "GraspWMS Privacy Protection Agreement" (hereinafter referred to as "this Privacy Agreement") will explain to you how GraspWMS collects, uses, and stores your personal information and what rights you have. Please read and understand the content of this Privacy Agreement carefully before using GraspWMS, and make the choices you think are appropriate according to the guidance of this Privacy Agreement. Once you start using GraspWMS products or services, it means that you have fully understood and agreed to all the contents of this Privacy Agreement. This Privacy Agreement applies to the functions and services of GraspWMS products themselves (including the software itself and mini-programs), and does not apply to products or services provided by any other third parties. You should fully understand the product functions and privacy protection policies of third-party services before choosing to use them. Relevant Definitions: • GraspWMS: GraspWMS is a mobile inventory, sales, and financial management tool provided by Chengdu Renwoxing Software Co., Ltd. GraspWMS products and services specifically refer to the GraspWMS client (including iOS and Android application versions) and the inventory, sales, and financial management application services it provides. • User: Refers to an individual or organization that purchases, registers, logs in, uses GraspWMS products and services, and obtains management rights, including but not limited to legal persons, government agencies, other organizations, partnerships, or individual industrial and commercial households (hereinafter referred to as "users"). Users can create their enterprise accounts (virtual workspaces created and managed by users, where multiple people can join and use GraspWMS services together) through GraspWMS and invite and authorize individual users to join their work platforms as their end users. Hereinafter, "you" or "user" is used. • Personal Information: Refers to various information that can identify the identity of a natural person alone or in combination with other information recorded electronically or in other ways, including but not limited to the name, phone number, and name of the affiliated enterprise of a natural person. • Sensitive Personal Information: Refers to personal information that may endanger personal and property safety, easily cause damage to personal reputation, physical and mental health, or discriminatory treatment if leaked, illegally provided, or misused. It includes the name, mobile phone number, and name of the affiliated enterprise of a natural person. • Deletion of Personal Information: Refers to the behavior of removing personal information from the systems involved in realizing daily business functions, making it in an unsearchable and inaccessible state. • Unique Device Identifier: (Exclusive ID or UUID) refers to a string of characters programmed into the device by the device manufacturer, which can be used to uniquely identify the corresponding device (such as the IMEI number of a mobile phone). • IP Address: Each device connected to the Internet is assigned a number called the Internet Protocol (IP) address. These numbers are usually assigned according to geographical regions. IP addresses can usually be used to identify the location of a device when it is connected to the Internet. • GPS: The abbreviation of Global Positioning System. The GPS signal receiver on the device you use can receive signals from GPS satellites and calculate your position using the transmitted information. • WIFI: Wi-Fi is a technology that allows electronic devices to connect to a wireless local area network (WLAN), usually referred to as a wireless network.

I. What Types of Information Do We Collect?

  • To provide you with services, optimize our services, and ensure account security, GraspWMS will collect the information you actively provide, authorize, or generate during registration and use of the services in the following ways:
  • 1.1 Account Registration Information: To facilitate your login to GraspWMS to obtain services, your mobile phone number may need to be provided by your company or yourself.
  • 1.2 When you use GraspWMS services, to provide you with GraspWMS products and services, maintain the normal operation of our services, improve and optimize our service experience, and ensure your account security, we will collect the following information:
  • 1.2.1 Device Information: Based on the device model and permissions granted during the installation and use of GraspWMS services, we will collect device-related information used by GraspWMS services, including the unique device identifier, GraspWMS software version number, and device accelerators (including gravity sensors).
  • 1.2.2 Log Information: When you use GraspWMS services, we will collect log information about your service use, including the way, type, and status of network access, network quality data, operation logs, and service log information (including the website information you view in GraspWMS and service failure information).
  • 1.3 Before users upload and manage the personal information of operator users' names and mobile phone numbers, please ensure that you have obtained the explicit consent of the operator users in advance. Only collect the necessary end-user information to achieve operational and functional purposes, and fully inform the end-users about the purpose, scope, and usage method of data collection.
  • 1.4 Currently, the main information required by GraspWMS and its uses are as follows:
  • 1) Please allow "GraspWMS" to access your location; otherwise, you will not be able to use the location check-in function.
  • 2) Please allow "GraspWMS" to access your photo album; otherwise, you will not be able to select product file pictures.
  • 3) Please allow "GraspWMS" to access your camera; otherwise, you will not be able to use the scanning function.
  • 4) Please allow "GraspWMS" to access your Bluetooth; otherwise, you will not be able to use the printer connection function.
  • 5) Please allow "GraspWMS" to access your dialing; otherwise, you will not be able to use the calling function.
  • 6) Please allow "GraspWMS" to access your storage; otherwise, you will not be able to use the offline order creation and product upgrade functions.
  • 7) Please allow "GraspWMS" to access your software installation list; otherwise, you will not be able to use the QQ and WeChat sharing functions.
  • 8) Please allow "GraspWMS" to access your MAC information; otherwise, it will cause the number of registered users to exceed the limit and the software to fail to log in normally.
  • 9) Please allow "GraspWMS" to access your device ID; otherwise, it will cause the number of registered users to exceed the limit and the software to fail to log in normally.
  • The above additional functions may require you to enable access permissions for your location (location information), camera (camera), photo album (picture library), and storage (internal storage) in your device to collect and use the information involved in these functions. You can view the enabled status of these permissions item by item in the "Settings" of your device (Android system: Settings - Apps & Notifications - Permission Manager - GraspWMS, IOS system: Settings - Privacy), and you can decide to turn these permissions on or off at any time. Please note that enabling these permissions means that you authorize us to collect and use this personal information to achieve the above functions. Disabling the permissions means that you cancel these authorizations, and we will no longer continue to collect and use this personal information, nor can we provide you with the functions corresponding to these authorizations. Your decision to disable the permissions will not affect the processing of personal information based on your previous authorizations. Please understand that the functions and services we provide to you are constantly updated and developed. If a certain function or service is not mentioned above and requires collecting your information, we will explain the content, scope, and purpose of information collection to you through page prompts, interaction processes, or website announcements to obtain your consent.

    • II. How Do We Store This Information?

  • We strictly abide by the provisions of laws and regulations and agreements with users and use the collected information for the following purposes. If we use your information beyond the following purposes, we will explain it to you again and obtain your consent.
  • 1. Location of Information Storage: Enterprise data is stored in your GraspWMS business data server. GraspWMS and its affiliated parties will store the personal information collected and generated in China within the Chinese mainland in accordance with the provisions of laws and regulations.
  • 2. Duration of Information Storage: Generally, we only retain your personal information for the time necessary to achieve the purpose. For user public cloud data, after the expiration of the service period you purchased, GraspWMS and its affiliated parties will delete or anonymize it within 7 days. When our products or services stop operating, we will notify you in the form of push notifications or announcements, delete your personal information or anonymize it within a reasonable period, and immediately stop collecting personal information.

    • III. How Do We Protect This Information?

  • 1. We strive to provide protection for users' information security to prevent information loss, improper use, unauthorized access, or disclosure.
  • 2. We will use various security protection measures within a reasonable security level to ensure information security. This includes using encryption technology and anonymization processing methods to protect your personal information.
  • 3. We have established special management systems, processes, and organizations to ensure information security. We strictly limit the scope of personnel accessing information, require them to sign a "confidentiality agreement" with the company and abide by confidentiality obligations, and conduct audits.
  • 4. In the event of a personal information leakage security incident, we will legally activate the emergency response plan, prevent the expansion of the security incident, and inform you of the situation of the security incident, the possible impact on you, and the remedial measures we will take in the form of push notifications or announcements. We will also report the handling of personal information security incidents in accordance with the requirements of laws, regulations, and regulatory authorities.

    • IV. How Do We Use Information?

  • 1. We collect relevant information during your use of GraspWMS services to create and provide better services for GraspWMS users. We will use the collected information for the following purposes:
  • 1) Security Assurance: To ensure the security of you and all GraspWMS users, we will use relevant information to assist in enhancing the security and reliability of GraspWMS services, including detecting, preventing, and responding to fraud, abuse, illegal acts, security risks, and technical problems that may endanger GraspWMS, our users, or the public;
  • 2) Communication with You: We will use the collected information (involving the phone number you provided) to communicate directly with you. If we detect suspicious activities (including attempts to log in to your GraspWMS from an unusual location), we may send you a notification. We may also let you know about upcoming changes or improvements to GraspWMS. Or, we will conduct service follow-up visits.
  • 3) If we use your personal information beyond the purpose claimed at the time of collection and the scope of direct or reasonable association, we will inform you again and obtain your express consent before using your personal information.
  • 2. For the use of enterprise-controlled data, we will handle it in accordance with the user's decision and the relevant agreement with the user. This includes that the user has the right to decide which and how to display the relevant information of the end-users in GraspWMS.
  • 3. According to relevant laws, regulations, and national standards, we may collect and use your personal information without seeking your authorization and consent in the following cases:
  • 1) Directly related to national interests such as national security and national defense security; directly related to major public interests such as public safety, public health, and public knowledge;
  • 2) Directly related to criminal investigation, prosecution, trial, and execution of judgments;
  • 3) To safeguard the significant legal rights and interests of you or other individuals but it is difficult to obtain your consent;
  • 4) The personal information you have publicly disclosed to the public;
  • 5) Personal information collected from legally publicly disclosed information, including legal news reports and government information disclosure channels;
  • 6) Necessary for signing and fulfilling contracts as required by you;
  • 7) Necessary for maintaining the safe and stable operation of the provided products or services. This includes discovering and handling product or service failures;
  • 8) Other circumstances stipulated by laws and regulations.
  • 4. We do not use the personal privacy information you provide for user profiling and personalized display. Moreover, we do not share your personal information with any third-party enterprises.

    • V. User Rights

  • 1. Change Personal Information
  • When you need to update your personal information or find that there is an error in the personal information stored by GraspWMS, you have the right to correct or update it. You can contact us to modify your personal information.
  • 2. Delete Personal Information. If you wish to delete personal information, you can contact us to delete your personal information in the management background. After the user deletes your personal information, we will delete or anonymize your personal information.
  • 3. Cancel Account. If you wish to cancel your account, you can contact us. We will delete your personal account in the management background. After deleting your personal account, your data will be deleted or anonymized synchronously. After canceling the account, we will stop providing services to you.

    • VI. Information Sharing and External Provision

  • 1. We will not share or transfer your personal information to third parties.
  • 2. We will not publicly disclose the collected personal information. If it is necessary to publicly disclose it, we will inform you of the purpose of this public disclosure, the type of disclosed information, and the possible sensitive information involved, and obtain your express consent. It should be noted that how to disclose or share the information of end-users in the user work platform they join is determined and managed by the user.
  • 3. With the continuous development of our business, there may be transactions such as mergers, acquisitions, and asset transfers. We will inform you of the relevant situation and continue to protect your personal information in accordance with the laws, regulations, and standards not lower than those required by this guide or require the new controller to continue to protect it.
  • 4. We may disclose your personal information based on legal requirements or law enforcement requirements of relevant departments.

    • VII. Third-Party SDKs Used by the System

  • The following lists the specific authorized partners and provides the privacy policy links of the third parties. We recommend that you read the privacy policies of the third parties:
  • 1. Baidu Maps
  • To enable users to upload location information within the application and mark location information in photo watermarks, so that enterprise management can count the field work information of salespersons, we use the Baidu Maps SDK. This SDK needs to read your device's network permissions, phone status information permissions, location information, and read and write storage permissions. For details, please read Baidu Privacy Policy General Rules
  • 2. AutoNavi Maps
  • To enable users to upload location information within the application and mark location information in photo watermarks, so that enterprise management can count the field work information of salespersons, we use the AutoNavi Maps SDK. This SDK needs to read your device's location information (latitude and longitude, precise location, coarse location), device information [such as IP address, GNSS information, WiFi status, WiFi parameters, WiFi list, SSID, BSSID, base station information, WiFi signal strength information, Bluetooth information, sensor information (vector, acceleration, pressure), device signal strength information, external storage directory], device identification information (IDFA, OAID), current application information (application name, application version number), and device parameters and system information (system properties, device model, operating system, carrier information). For details, please read AutoNavi Maps Open Platform Privacy Policy
  • 3. Tencent Bugly
  • To monitor the crash and page exception problems of this client and provide you with better services, we use the Tencent Bugly SDK. This SDK needs to read your device's network permissions, phone status information permissions, and read and write storage permissions. For details, please read Bugly SDK Privacy Protection Statement
  • 4. GeTui
  • To ensure the timely delivery of approval messages, we use the GeTui SDK. This SDK needs to read your device's device information, application list information, network information, and location-related information. For details, please read GeTui User Privacy Policy
  • 5. TBS/Tencent Browser/Tencent X5 Browser/Tencent Browser Service
  • To open files such as word and Excel within the application for viewing, we use the Tencent Browser Service SDK. This SDK needs to read your device's device information (device model, operating system, CPU type), application information (host application package name, version number), Wi-Fi status and parameters, location information, nearby Wi-Fi, CellID, BSSID related information. For details, please read Tencent Browser Service X5 Web Engine SDK Personal Information Protection Rules
  • 6. iFlytek Speech Dictation
  • To achieve fast entry of document content through speech recognition within the application, we use the iFlytek Speech Dictation SDK. This SDK needs to read your device's device number (IMEI number), device code (IMSI code), IP address, Android ID (Android unique device ID to provide capability authorization service), China Mobile UAID (Android devices only), OAID (Android devices only), UUID (iOS devices only), mac address (Linux devices only), text, and operation log related information. For details, please read Online Speech Dictation SDK Privacy Policy
  • 7. WeChat Open SDK
  • To enable the sharing of business documents to WeChat mini-programs, we use the WeChat Open SDK. This SDK needs to read your device's actively selected pictures or content, WeChat avatar, and WeChat nickname related information. For details, please read WeChat Open SDK Personal Information Processing Rules
  • 8. Huawei Unified Scanning SDK
  • To enable the application to provide code generation or scanning capabilities, we use the Huawei Unified Scanning SDK. This SDK needs to read your device's read storage (media and file) permissions, camera permissions, and network permissions. For details, please read Huawei Unified Scanning SDK Privacy Statement

    • VIII. Protection of Minors

  • We attach great importance to the protection of minors' personal information. According to the provisions of relevant laws and regulations, if you are a minor, you should obtain the written consent of your parents or legal guardians in advance before using GraspWMS services. If you are the guardian of a minor and have any questions about the personal information of the minor you are guardianship, please contact us through the contact information in Section X.

  • 2) Changes in the storage location of data;
  • 3) Changes in the use rules of users' personal information;
  • 4) Changes in our contact information and complaint channels;
  • 5) Other changes that may affect the security of users' personal information or users' privacy rights;
  • 6) Other change situations.
  • 2. The revision process of our Privacy Agreement is as follows:

  • 3. After this Privacy Agreement is revised, we will display the revised Privacy Agreement to you in the form of push notifications or pop-up windows. In this case, if you continue to use our products or services, it means that you agree to be bound by the revised Privacy Agreement.
  • 4. This Privacy Agreement will come into effect on the date of release. Unless GraspWMS makes adjustments to this Privacy Agreement according to relevant laws and regulations and releases a new Privacy Agreement, this Privacy Agreement will remain valid.
